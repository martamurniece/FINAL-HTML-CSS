Nike : Park Verademing

Concept Proposal



This project was executed in a group of three UX designers for Nike within the context of the Hague University of Applied Sciences.

The main goal was to create a concept in 15 weeks which would empower and support the community of park of Veradardening in the Hague .

Our focus was on the research and on concept development for which we created an IOS app onboarding process and a 3D prototype to outline proposed functionalities of the concept.
